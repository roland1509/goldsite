Uncut Diamond

Uncut diamonds, also known as rough or raw diamonds, are diamonds in their natural state, untouched by the cutting and polishing processes that transform them into the sparkling gems commonly seen in jewelry. These diamonds are extracted directly from the earth and retain their original, rugged appearance, which many find uniquely appealing.

Gold Ore

Gold ore is a naturally occurring material from which gold can be extracted. At Kirsten Mining Company, we specialize in mining high-quality gold ore, which is found in various forms such as quartz gold ore, silver-gold ore, and iron oxide copper gold ore. The extraction process involves crushing the ore, followed by grinding and various separation techniques to isolate the gold.

Natural Diamond

Natural diamonds are formed deep within the Earth’s mantle under extreme pressure and temperature over millions of years. These precious stones are composed of carbon atoms arranged in a crystal lattice structure, making them the hardest known natural substance. At Kirsten Mining Company, we pride ourselves on sourcing high-quality natural diamonds through ethical and sustainable mining practices.
